Review of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a combination of technologies that allows computers to perform a wide range of sophisticated operations, such as seeing, understanding, and translating spoken and written language, analyzing data, making suggestions, and so on. AI is the driving force behind current computer innovation, creating value for both individuals and enterprise.

Review of Neurotechnology

Neurotechnology refers to any method or technological equipment that interacts with the nervous system to monitor or regulate brain activity. Common design aims for neurotechnologies include using brain activity readings to control external devices such as neuroprosthetics, modifying neural activity through neuromodulation to heal or restore function impacted by neurological disorders, or boosting cognitive capacities. In addition to their medicinal or commercial applications, neurotechnologies are effective research tools for advancing basic neuroscience knowledge.
